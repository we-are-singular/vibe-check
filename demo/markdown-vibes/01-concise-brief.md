# Concise product brief

## The idea

Give teams one calm place to review product direction before work begins.

- Capture the decision in plain language.
- Name the owner and the next step.
- Keep the discussion focused on the outcome.

## Why it helps

A short brief makes trade-offs visible without turning planning into paperwork.
