# Narrative product brief

A customer arrives with a question and leaves with a confident next step.

## What changes

Instead of asking people to translate a dashboard, we explain the signal in context:

1. What happened?
2. Why does it matter now?
3. What should the team do next?

> Good product writing gives people enough context to act.

## The promise

Every decision should feel understandable to the person responsible for it.
