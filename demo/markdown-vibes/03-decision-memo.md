# Decision memo

## Recommendation

Launch the smallest useful workflow, then measure whether it reduces review time.

## Evidence

| Signal | What we observed |
| --- | --- |
| Review time | Teams spend too long reconciling competing notes. |
| Ownership | Decisions often lose a clear owner after the meeting. |
| Confidence | People move faster when the next action is explicit. |

## Guardrails

- Keep the first release narrow.
- Review the evidence after two weeks.
- Change course when the signal disagrees with the assumption.
